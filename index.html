Our Approach

Not a marketplace.
Accountable.

NOVA works differently. You tell us what you need. We pick the people, manage the work, and check everything before it reaches you. If something is not right, we fix it on our time, not yours.

Big companies have a department for every one of these jobs. Small businesses have nobody. NOVA is that department, at a price built for your size. That is the gap we exist to close.

"Can't I just use AI for this?"

For small things, you should. But AI is a tool, and a tool still needs you: you run it, you check its work, you fix it when it is wrong. It makes tasks faster. It does not take the job off your plate. You are still the one doing the job.

NOVA takes the job itself, with the scope agreed in writing before we start. Your books close every month without you asking. Your site keeps working. Your reports arrive finished. Real people do the work, learn your business over time, and fix it at our cost when something is off. That is the difference in one line: a tool helps you do the job. NOVA does the job.

"AI raised the ceiling on what you can do yourself. It did nothing about whether you have the time to do it."
